Insight from the Choropleth

As depicted in the Choropleth map, the distribution of electric vehicles across counties in Washington State shows a distinct concentration around the Seattle metropolitan area. Counties near this metropolitan region, such as King County, exhibit significantly higher rates of electric vehicle adoption, evident from the considerable count of 80,637 electric vehicles registered.

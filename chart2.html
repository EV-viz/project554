BEVs VS PHEVs

Battery electric vehicle (BEVs) are powered entirely by electricity, while Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) are powered by a fuel-based internal combustion engine, as well as an electric motor with a battery that's rechargeable with an external plug.
